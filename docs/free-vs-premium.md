# Free vs Premium

## Free access

- Maximum five saved watchlist items.
- Limited AI insights.
- Delayed demonstration alerts.
- Limited AI Daily Report.
- Premium cards visible but locked.
- Scenario planner may show a preview or partial results.

## Premium access

- Unlimited watchlists.
- Complete AI insights.
- Smart Alerts.
- Full scenario planner.
- Broker comparison preview unlocked.
- Full AI Daily Report.
- Complete Practice Investing experience.

## Prototype rules

- Use a visible developer account switcher for Free and Premium views.
- Do not process real payments.
- Upgrade flow is demonstration-only.
- Premium visuals should use purple/blue accents without overpowering financial data.
